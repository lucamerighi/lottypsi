My Therapeutic Approach

My practice is built on evidence-based therapeutic approaches tailored to each individual's unique needs and circumstances.

Philosophy of Care

I believe in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences freely. My approach is collaborative and client-centered, honoring your unique perspective and goals.

I view therapy as a partnership where we work together to identify patterns, develop insights, and build practical skills to navigate life's challenges. You are the expert on your life, and my role is to provide guidance, support, and evidence-based strategies to help you reach your goals.

Whether you're seeking relief from specific symptoms, working through a difficult transition, or pursuing personal growth, I tailor my approach to meet you where you are and support your journey toward positive change.

Therapeutic Methods

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Helps identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors that contribute to psychological distress.

Mindfulness-Based Approaches

Cultivates awareness of the present moment to reduce stress and increase psychological flexibility.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Explores how past experiences may influence current patterns of thought, emotion, and behavior.

Solution-Focused Brief Therapy

Emphasizes identifying solutions and setting goals rather than dwelling on problems.

Trauma-Informed Care

Recognizes the impact of trauma and prioritizes safety, trust, and empowerment in the therapeutic process.
